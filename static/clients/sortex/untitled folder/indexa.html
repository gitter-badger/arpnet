Anhui Huoju Machine-Building Co.,Ltd.located in economic and technical development zone of Hefei ,Anhui, is an high- tech enterprise mainly making machines used for agricultural.
　　Our company has accumulated more than ten years experience of mechanical production, and passed the ISO9001:2000 quality management system certification. The products are listed in National Science And Technology Department as "State-Level Spark Program Projects". Most machines are respectively exported to Malaysia、 Vietnam、Cambodia、 Ukraine、 Angola、 Zimbabwe、Thailand、Russia 、Nigeria and other countries ,examination shows that our technology have reached the international advanced level.
　　In recent years, relying on our own advantages of mechanical manufacturing platform, adhered to the science and technology as a guide, has a special develop and research team with experts who consummate skill & profuse experience especially in mechanical vision and image processing. We take intellectual property to produce a new generation "Torch" brand intelligent and high-end color CCD (5000 pixel×3( red、green、blue three colors)) color sorters. At the same time, we have close cooperation with many key domestic、foreign universities and research institutes, then to fully understand and master the latest industry trends and technology of color sorting machine, and to ensure products always in the leading position.
　　At present, our company has produced Series Precision and High speed CCD Color Sorters for distinguishing products via color selection. Our sorter could choose widely rang of products, such as nuts, seeds, dehydrated vegetables, food, ore, salt, plastic and so on. We will keep our eyes to catch the pulse of market and make more products to meet different clients’ needs. 
　　With advanced technology and high-quality equipments as well as dedicated after-sale service team, Our business purpose is"based on good faith, leading with good technology"; quality is our life and service is our soul; "keeping with honesty and innovation; modesty and profound accumulation" is our attitude; "customer is our god" is our business philosophy.
　　We are warmly looking forward to win-win cooperation from home and abroad friends，together to create a brilliant future.
